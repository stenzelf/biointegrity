Biointegrity data viewer

This viewer displays global functional biosphere integrity raster datasets from 1600 to 2014, including risk for biosphere destabilization (EcoRisk), human appropriation of net primary production (HANPP), and derived planetary boundary metrics. Use the selectors below to explore the different variables and years and download data as GeoTIFF files.

Suggested citation:
Stenzel et al. 2025 https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.15806793
