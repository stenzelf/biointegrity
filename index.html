Biointegrity data viewer

This viewer displays global functional biosphere integrity raster datasets from 1600 to 2014, including risk for biosphere destabilization (EcoRisk), human appropriation of net primary production (HANPP), and derived planetary boundary metrics developed in Stenzel et al. 2025: Breaching planetary boundaries. The metrics are based on simulations with the vegetation model LPJmL. The thresholds determining the risk-level for the planetary boundary are (safe/intermediate; intermediate/high risk): HANPPHol (0.05; 0.23), EcoRisk (0.35; 0.55).
Use the selectors below to explore the different variables and years and download data as GeoTIFF files.

Suggested data citation:
Stenzel (2025)
